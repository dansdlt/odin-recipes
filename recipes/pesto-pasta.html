PESTO PASTA

Description

A zesty pasta dish that will put a smile in your stomach.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons pesto
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan chesse
  • 1 (16 oz) package pasta
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste

Directions

Step 1

Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling water until done. Drain.

Step 2

Meanwhile, heat the oil in a frying pan over medium low heat. Add pesto, onion, and salt and pepper. Cook About five minutes; or until onions are soft.

Step 3

In a large bowl, mix pesto mixture into pasta. Stir in grated cheese.

Step 4

Enjoy!
